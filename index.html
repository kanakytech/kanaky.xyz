About

Building the digital infrastructure of the Pacific.

Kanaky Tech is a digital technology firm founded in New Caledonia in 2024. We specialise in the intersection of artificial intelligence, decentralised systems and institutional digital transformation.

Our approach is pragmatic and sovereignty-first: we build tools that serve local needs, run on infrastructure that organisations control, and generate value that stays in the region.

We work with governments, agencies and enterprises across the Pacific — with proven frameworks built on the ground.
